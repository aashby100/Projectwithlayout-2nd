Alaska

Anchorage & Fairbanks

I traveled to Alaska on December 27-January 4, 2019 for New Years. I went to both Anchorage and Fairbanks. I was able to view the wonderful sights of both the glaciers and the northern lights. I also had the opportunity to ride a snowmachine, go dog sledding, and ice fishing!
